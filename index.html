Privacy Policy

Applies to our Android apps: 52‑Cards AI Mind Reader (package com.creativewandering.mindreader52) and The Mystical 21 Card Trick (package com.creativewandering.mindreader21).

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Overview

Creative Wandering (“we”, “us”) values your privacy. Our magic‑trick apps are simple, offline experiences. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, share, or sell your personal data.

What data we collect

  • Personal data: None collected.
  • Sensitive data: None collected.
  • Usage/analytics: None collected. We do not use third‑party analytics SDKs.
  • Advertising: No ads.

If this policy ever changes to include data collection, we will update this page and the Google Play Data Safety form accordingly.

Device permissions

Our current releases do not intentionally request sensitive permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location). Basic Android permissions required by the app framework may be present to run the app, but the apps do not transmit personal information to our servers (we do not operate any backend for these apps).

Payments & refunds

  • All purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive your payment details.
  • Refunds are handled per the Google Play refund policy. If you need help, contact us and include your order number.

Children’s privacy

Our apps are general‑audience entertainment and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

While these apps do not collect personal data, we still aim to follow reasonable practices to keep the apps safe. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and the apps are provided “as is.”

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
