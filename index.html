Our Philosophy

These apps perform simple, on-device card effects. We designed them to avoid collecting personal data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. There is no account system, no analytics SDK, and no ads inside the apps.

Network Access

Permissions

The apps use only basic permissions required for normal operation (e.g., internet to open links). No sensitive permissions (e.g., contacts, location, microphone) are requested.

Children’s Privacy

The apps are suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
